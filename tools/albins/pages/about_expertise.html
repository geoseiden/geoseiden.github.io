Our Story Meet Our Team

From Heritage to Innovation

Founded in 1985 by master craftsman Robert Chen, LuxeCraft began as a small family workshop specializing in traditional furniture restoration. What started with a passion for preserving craftsmanship has evolved into the premier destination for luxury corporate upholstery services.

Our journey from traditional upholstery to corporate excellence was driven by a simple vision: "Elevated Comfort" in professional environments. We recognized that exceptional workspaces inspire exceptional performance.

Today, LuxeCraft stands at the intersection of artisanal heritage and modern innovation, serving Fortune 500 companies, luxury hotels, and discerning professionals who understand that quality is never an accident.

Four Decades of Excellence

From humble beginnings to industry leadership, our journey reflects continuous growth and unwavering commitment to quality.
